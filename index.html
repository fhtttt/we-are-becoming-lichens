II · How a Lichen Spreads

Three bets on how to carry a relationship across changing terrain.

A lichen is not one organism but a 400-million-year-old partnership — a fungus and an alga. To reproduce, it has to decide: do the partners travel together, or do they part ways and hope to meet again?
Mode 01

Soredia — bundles of both

Algae
Fungus
Soredium (both)
Both partners travel together. Each soredium is a ready-made bundle — algal cells wrapped in fungal threads. Lands anywhere, grows immediately.
Mode 02

Isidia — broken-off branches

Algae
Fungus
Isidium (both)
Both partners travel together. A coral-like outgrowth carrying a full cross-section of the lichen. Heavier than soredia — moves shorter distances, but identical to the parent.
Mode 03

Ascospores — fungus alone

Spore (fungus only)
Free alga
Encounter
Only the fungus disperses. Ascospores carry no algae and must find a compatible partner already living in the new place. Risky — no guarantees.
III · Three Mobilities — placeholder

The same three strategies, written in human movement.

If a lichen's reproduction is a bet on how to carry a relationship across terrain, then contemporary mobility is doing the same arithmetic at planetary scale.
Parallel 01 · ↔ Soredia

Digital nomads

Both partners travel · self-contained bundles

A person, a laptop, a network of tools — moving together as a ready-made unit. Lands anywhere with bandwidth and begins immediately. Light, high-dispersal, low ties to substrate.

Parallel 02 · ↔ Isidia

Climate migration

Whole communities — moving as a piece of the parent

Families, villages, livelihoods carried together as a coral-like fragment of an existing place. Shorter distances. The relationship survives intact, but the terrain it lands on is new.

Parallel 03 · ↔ Ascospores

Global refugees

One partner travels · must find the other on arrival

Forced dispersal without the supporting ecology. A person arrives carrying only themselves, and must locate work, kin, language, legal status — partners they must capture in a new place. The riskiest bet.
